I am a JSPS postdoctoral fellow at Graduate School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Tokyo.

I am primarily interested in arithmetic geometry, in particular, questions in p-adic Hodge theory and p-adic cohomology theories.

Previously, I was a postdoc in Lille. Before that, I was a doctoral student at Institut de Mathématiques de Bordeaux under the supervision of Denis Benois and Nicola Mazzari. Before joining the doctoral program, I was an ALGANT master student in Leiden and Bordeaux. Even before that, I was a software engineer.
